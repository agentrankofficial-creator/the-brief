What Happened

Analysis · The Containment Window
Mythos represents the first credible AI that can systematically find vulnerabilities faster than defenders can patch them. This inverts the attacker-defender dynamic: historically, defenders had time after disclosure; now they may not. The 18-month window before open-source equivalents appear is the crucial containment period. Project Glasswing is Anthropic's bet: if you give defenders the same tool attackers will soon have, you create a brief head start. Whether 40 organizations is enough to patch "every major OS and browser" in 18 months is deeply uncertain. The vulnerability was described as spanning decades-old flaws that human-led security tests missed repeatedly — suggesting the attack surface is wider than anyone assumed.
